What does the combined carpet cleaning and pest control service include?

Our combined service covers professional carpet cleaning using hot water extraction, followed by a pest control treatment applied throughout the property. For most end of lease jobs, the package includes:

  • Hot water extraction carpet cleaning - all carpeted rooms, hallways, and stairs
  • Flea treatment as standard for properties with pets
  • General pest treatment for common Gold Coast pests
  • Tax invoice provided for both services - accepted by property managers
  • Same-day scheduling available for urgent end of lease bookings

Why book carpet cleaning and pest control together?

Booking carpet cleaning and pest control as a combined service on the Gold Coast makes practical sense for several reasons. It reduces the number of tradespeople you need to coordinate, means the property only needs to be vacated once, and the combined cost is typically lower than booking each service separately.

For tenants moving out of a rental, it also simplifies the end of lease process - one invoice covering both requirements, which makes bond claims straightforward.

Is flea treatment included?

Yes. Flea treatment is included as standard in our carpet cleaning and pest control package. This is by far the most common requirement we see on the Gold Coast, particularly for rental properties with dogs or cats.

If your carpets also have visible pet staining or heavy odour from urine, you may want to add our pet stain and odour carpet cleaning treatment - this is a separate process that targets odour residues at the fibre level, not just surface cleaning.

Is this service suitable for end of lease?

Yes - it's one of our most popular bond and end of lease carpet cleaning services on the Gold Coast. Property managers and real estate agents routinely request a combined carpet and pest certificate for end of lease sign-off, particularly for properties with pets.

We provide a tax invoice for the combined service that satisfies most real estate agencies. If your property manager has a specific requirement, let us know when you book and we'll confirm we can meet it.

What pest control is covered?

Our standard pest control component covers flea treatment and general perimeter pest treatment for common Gold Coast insects. We do not provide licensed pest control for termites, rodents, or specialised infestations - those require a separate licensed pest control technician.

If you have specific pest requirements beyond flea treatment, contact us before booking so we can confirm the scope of the service.
