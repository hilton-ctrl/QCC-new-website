Pet Stain and Odour Treatment

Pet stain and odour carpet cleaning targets urine, vomit, faeces, and wet-dog smell at the fibre level - not just the surface. Standard carpet cleaning is not designed to remove pet odour. Specialist treatment breaks down odour-causing bacteria and treats the stain residue that causes yellowing and odour recurrence.

This service is used by pet owners who want to refresh their carpets, by tenants preparing for bond inspections, and by landlords managing pet-affected rental properties. Learn more about pet stain and odour carpet cleaning →

Advanced Spot and Stain Treatment

Advanced spot and stain treatment is for stains that standard carpet cleaning cannot remove - red wine, coffee, blood, rust, ink, makeup, and other set-in stains. Before treating, we assess the stain type, the carpet fibre, and the expected outcome so there are no surprises.

Not every stain can be fully removed. Fibre type, stain age, and whether the stain was previously treated all affect the result. We will always tell you what to expect before we start. Learn more about advanced stain treatment →

Couch and Upholstery Cleaning

Couch and upholstery cleaning restores fabric sofas, sectionals, chairs, and lounges. We clean microfibre, velvet, linen, cotton, and synthetic blends. For pet-affected upholstery, pet odour treatment is applied at the same time as the fabric clean.

"Couch cleaning" is one of the highest-volume carpet-related search terms in Queensland at 1,600 searches per month locally. It is also one of the most visibly satisfying services we provide. Learn more about couch and upholstery cleaning →
