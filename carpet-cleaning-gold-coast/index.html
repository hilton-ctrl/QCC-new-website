Hot water extraction carpet cleaning

We use truck-mounted and portable hot water extraction equipment - the most effective method for deep cleaning residential carpet on the Gold Coast. Hot water extraction works by injecting hot water and a cleaning agent deep into the carpet pile, then immediately extracting it along with dissolved dirt, allergens, bacteria, and staining.

Compared to dry cleaning or steam cleaning, hot water extraction removes significantly more embedded soiling. It's the method recommended by most carpet manufacturers for routine maintenance and deep cleaning.

  • Penetrates deep into the carpet pile - not just surface cleaning
  • Removes allergens, dust mites, and bacteria as well as visible dirt
  • Safe for all standard residential carpet types
  • Carpets dry within 2-4 hours in normal Gold Coast conditions

Bond carpet cleaning and end of lease carpet cleaning is one of our most common services across the Gold Coast. Property managers and real estate agents have specific requirements for carpet condition at the end of a tenancy, and a professional clean with a tax invoice is the standard expectation.

We provide tax invoices for all bond carpet cleaning that are accepted by the major Gold Coast real estate agencies. We can also combine carpet cleaning with pest control and flea treatment for a single-visit end of lease solution.

Pet stain and odour carpet cleaning Gold Coast

Gold Coast homes with dogs and cats frequently need specialist pet stain and odour carpet cleaning that goes beyond a standard clean. Dog and cat urine in particular soaks through the carpet pile and into the backing and underlay - a surface clean doesn't resolve the odour.

We apply an enzyme-based treatment that breaks down urine crystals and odour-causing residues at the source. The result is a genuinely fresh carpet, not just a masked smell.

Couch and upholstery cleaning Gold Coast
