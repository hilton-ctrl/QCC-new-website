What's Included in Bond Carpet Cleaning?

Bond carpet cleaning covers all carpeted areas in the property using truck-mounted hot water extraction - the same equipment and method required by most real estate agencies and property managers on the Gold Coast.

  • Hot water extraction cleaning of all carpeted rooms, hallways, and stairs
  • Spot treatment for visible stains included as standard
  • Tax invoice provided on the day - accepted by real estate agents for bond purposes
  • Pet stain and odour treatment available as an add-on if required
  • Pest control and flea treatment available in the same visit
  • Receipts issued in the business name of Quick Carpet Cleaners

We do not make "bond guaranteed" claims because no cleaning company can guarantee a bond outcome - that decision sits with the property manager and the incoming inspection. What we do guarantee is that the cleaning meets the industry standard required for end of lease inspections.

Do You Provide Receipts for Property Managers?

Yes. We provide a tax invoice on the day of the clean that includes the business name, ABN, date of service, property address, and services performed. This is the documentation that real estate agents and property managers require for bond claim purposes in Queensland.

If your property manager requires a specific format or additional information on the invoice, let us know when you book and we will accommodate it where possible.

Can You Do Carpet Cleaning and Pest Control Together for End of Lease?

Yes. Combined end of lease carpet cleaning and pest control is one of our most popular services on the Gold Coast. It is commonly required as a condition of the lease for tenants who have kept pets in the property.

The pest control component includes a flea treatment applied after the carpet clean. Both services are completed in a single visit and covered on the same tax invoice. This saves you the cost and coordination of managing two separate contractors. Learn more about carpet cleaning and pest control →

What If We Have Pets - Can You Treat Pet Stains Too?

Yes. If the property has pet staining or pet odour, specialist pet stain and odour treatment can be added to the bond carpet clean in the same visit. Standard bond carpet cleaning will not remove embedded pet odour - the treatment needs to penetrate the carpet fibre and backing to neutralise the uric acid residue.

If you are unsure whether pet treatment is needed, call us and we can advise based on the type of staining described. Learn more about pet stain and odour removal →

How Far in Advance Should I Book?

We recommend booking as early as possible once you know your move-out date. End of month is the busiest period for bond carpet cleaning on the Gold Coast, particularly Friday to Sunday. Booking 1-2 weeks in advance ensures you get the slot you need.

If you have a tight deadline, call us directly - we accommodate last-minute bookings where our schedule allows.
