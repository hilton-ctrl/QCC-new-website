4.  **Prevent Orphaned Pages:** Ensure each page is linked from:
    *   The Main Services Page [3].
    *   The Parent "Northern Gold Coast" Hub Page [3, 13].
    *   A dedicated "Service Areas" hub page in the navigation menu [3].
    *   At least 3 contextually relevant internal links pointing to it [3].

## SECTION 3: LAYOUT & CONVERSION DESIGN (THE "SQUINT TEST" BLUEPRINT)

Each generated page must pass the **"Squint Test"**—ensuring the primary calls to action are immediately obvious on mobile without zooming or scrolling [4].

### 3.1 Page Layout Sequence
1.  **Above the Fold (Hero Section) [5]:**
    *   **H1 Headline:** Outcome-based, establishing trade, location, and key problems handled [5].
        *   *Format:* `Need Premium Carpet Cleaning in {{ SUBURB }} Today? Fast, Dry-Within-Hours Steam Cleaning`
    *   **Subheadline:** Specifies property types and suburbs served in plain language [5].
        *   *Format:* `Professional carpet restoration for {{ SUBURB }} homes, luxury apartments, and commercial offices.`
    *   **Primary CTA:** Large, high-contrast, click-to-call button: `Call [Phone Number]` [5].
    *   **Secondary CTA:** High-contrast `Get a Free Quote` button [6].
    *   **Reassuring Nudge:** Small-text anchor below CTAs: `Licensed & Insured. Dry-Touch Technology. Southport & Northern Gold Coast locals.` [6]
    *   **Trust Row:** Badges highlighting core credentials [6]:
        *   `⭐ 4.9 Google Rating`
        *   `100% Satisfaction Guarantee`
        *   `Eco-Friendly & Pet Safe`
        *   `Local {{ SUBURB }} Technicians`
    *   **Visual Asset:** A real photo of the local technician, the carpet cleaning machine setup, or a branded van. No stock photos [6].
2.  **Mobile Conversion Layer [7]:**
    *   **Sticky Mobile Header:** Fixed at top, showing business logo and direct Click-to-Call icon [7].
    *   **Sticky Bottom CTA Bar:** Fixed footer strip scrolling with user: `Call Local Cleaner | Get Free Quote` [7].
3.  **Problem/Service Selection Cards [7]:**
    *   Interactive grid showcasing localized, high-intent solutions linking to deep service pages [7]:
        *   `Pet Stain Removal`
        *   `Deep Steam Cleaning`
        *   `End-of-Lease Restoration`
        *   `Upholstery & Rug Cleaning`
4.  **Shared Core Services Section (Consistent across all pages) [14]:**
    *   Detailed breakdowns of:
        *   **Steam carpet cleaning:** Deep extraction for optimal hygiene [14].
        *   **Pet stain removal:** Chemical breakdown of uric acid [14].
        *   **Odour treatment:** Sanitising and deodorising fibers [14].
        *   **End-of-lease carpet cleaning:** Bond-back guarantee specification [14].
        *   **Upholstery cleaning:** Safe cleaning for couches and chairs [14].
        *   **Commercial carpet cleaning:** Low-moisture and fast-drying options for businesses [14].
5.  **Local Trust & Testimonials [8]:**
    *   Place 3–6 real, localized customer reviews. Testimonials must reference suburb-specific pain points (e.g., "removed red mud after backyard construction in Pimpama" or "saved our luxury wool rug in Sanctuary Cove") [8, 14, 20].
6.  **Low-Friction Lead Capture Form [8]:**
    *   Limit to 3–5 fields: Name, Phone Number, Suburb, Core Issue (Dropdown: General Clean, Pet Stains, Bond Clean, Rugs/Upholstery), and optional "Upload Photo" [8, 9].
    *   **Form CTA:** Command-based button: `Send My Carpet Cleaning Request` [9].
7.  **Interactive FAQ Layer (AI Retrieval Optimization) [9]:**
    *   Direct, schema-compliant questions and answers structured for Google AI Overviews and Perplexity search [9]. Focus on suburb-specific anxieties [14].
8.  **"Super Footer" Navigation [10]:**
    *   Left Column: **Services** (Steam Carpet Cleaning, Pet Stain Treatment, Upholstery Cleaning, Rug Cleaning, Odour Sanitising) [10, 14].
    *   Right Column: **Nearby Service Areas** (Dynamic links to other 8 cluster suburbs) [10, 13].
