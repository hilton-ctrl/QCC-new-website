Why our quotes are fixed before we start

The most common complaint about carpet cleaning operators is price creep on the day. The quote over the phone is $120; the invoice ends up being $280 because "the carpet was worse than expected" or "treatment chemicals were extra."

We don't operate that way. The quote you get — over the phone or via the Talk to Us widget after we've seen photos — is the quote we honour at the door. The only situations where pricing changes:

  • You ask for additional work beyond the original quote — we tell you the new price before doing the extra work.
  • The scope is genuinely larger than what you described — we stop, show you, give you a revised fixed quote, and you decide whether to proceed.

What we never do: arrive at one price and present a different price on the invoice without your prior agreement.

What's included in every quote

  • Pre-vacuum (most operators skip this — it improves outcomes 20–30%)
  • Pre-treatment of high-traffic areas
  • Hot water extraction at 92°C
  • Edge detailing along skirting boards
  • Furniture moved and replaced (within reason)
  • Drying time advice and aftercare guidance
  • Fully ABN-registered tax invoice for property managers, tax records, and investment property deductions

Not included as standard (priced separately, always quoted upfront): pet urine treatment beyond standard cleaning, specialist stain treatment, heavy furniture moves requiring two people, very large homes (5-bedroom-plus) scoped by area.
