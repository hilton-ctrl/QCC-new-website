How the Cleaning Works

Hot water extraction cleaning — high-pressure water and solution injected into the carpet pile, then extracted along with dirt, allergens, and residue using powerful suction.

The extraction wand delivers hot water and cleaning solution under pressure and recovers the solution with powerful suction in a single pass.

Deep Fibre Extraction
